About 100SMILES Dental Care

Modern dentistry, decades of trusted care — delivered with compassion, clarity and comfort.

Our Story

100SMILES Dental Care has been part of the Razole community for over 30 years. Our team shares more than 50 years of combined experience in dental care, providing families with comfort, clarity and reliable treatment at every stage of life.

Whether it’s a child's first check-up or an adult’s long-term dental care, we strive to make every visit reassuring, gentle and positive. Our clinic blends modern equipment with a warm, friendly environment to help patients feel confident and cared for.

Trading Hours:
Mon–Fri 8:30am–6:30pm • Sat–Sun 8:30am–1pm

What We Do

We provide a full range of preventive, restorative and emergency dental services — from routine check-ups and cleanings to orthodontics, implants, cosmetic dentistry, root canal treatments and more.

Our dentists explain every diagnosis and treatment in clear, simple language so patients understand their options and feel confident about their care.

Why Choose 100SMILES?

  • Decades of trust: Serving Razole for over 30 years with returning families across generations.
  • Care for all ages: Child-friendly, adult-focused and senior-supportive dentistry.
  • Modern equipment: Digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, CAD/CAM restorations & more.
  • Clear communication: Transparent treatment options, outcomes and costs.
  • Preventive philosophy: Long-term oral health through regular care and education.

Our Promise

  • Compassion & Respect: Kindness and dignity for every patient.
  • Clinical Excellence: Safety-focused, evidence-based treatment.
  • Clear Guidance: You’ll always understand your options before treatment begins.
