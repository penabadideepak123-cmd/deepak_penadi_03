Annual Dental Check-Up*

Annual Dental Check-Up — comprehensive package

The Annual Dental Check-Up package gives a full picture of your oral health and includes:

  • A thorough examination of your overall dental health, including checking for any signs of illness, abnormalities, or discomfort.
  • Ensuring that your teeth are clean and free from plaque and tartar using professional scaling and polishing.
  • Digital imaging where needed to assess teeth & bone and detect issues not visible during the exam.
  • Evaluation for signs of decay, gum disease, wear and other conditions, plus personalised advice.
  • Discussion about diet and recommendations to maintain a healthy smile and overall wellbeing.
  • Additional diagnostic tests may be recommended based on age, history or specific clinical findings.
