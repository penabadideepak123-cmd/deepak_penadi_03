Orthodontics

Pediatric Dentistry

Dr Emily Carter

Dr Carter focuses on children’s dental health and works to make visits calm and positive. She supports families in building strong, lifelong oral hygiene habits for growing children.

General Dentistry

Dr Sloan provides general and restorative dentistry, including check-ups and treatments to restore and protect teeth. He focuses on long-term oral health and clear, practical advice for patients.
